How To Delete Instagram Account On Android | How to Download My Instagram Data

BDTM
Mar 18, 2022

 Learn how to permanently delete an Instagram account On Android or temporarily disable your account.


As worry over privacy and the amount of time we spend on social media grows, more individuals wonder how to delete an Instagram account. There's no disputing that Instagram is a fantastic medium for sharing personal information and experiences, promoting creative work, and even selling it directly. It's one of the most popular social networks on the planet, with more than a billion subscribers and significant potential for reaching a larger audience.


However, there are various reasons why you might choose to abandon the platform. It may be because you're worried that Meta (Facebook's new moniker, in case you missed it) possesses so much data. Maybe you spend too much time on social media and want to avoid the temptation of opening the app every time you look at your phone.


Whatever the cause, we'll show you how to delete an Instagram account and wipe all of your data and history from the platform. If you're sure you'd like to leave Instagram, you can erase it forever. If you're not sure you want to abandon Instagram permanently and lose all of your previous posts and Reels, you can temporarily disable it. We'll show you how to do both in this article with step-by-step instructions.





How To Delete Instagram Account On Android


Log in to your Instagram account.


The profile logo can be tapped.


Select the Menu (three-line) option in the top-right corner.


Now select Settings.


Click Help Center from the "Help" menu.


Choose between Chrome and any other browser.


Select Managing Your Account from the drop-down menu.


Select 'Delete Your Account' from the drop-down menu.


"How can I remove my account?" will appear.


Now select "Delete your account page" from the drop-down menu.


Select Chrome once more and log in to your account.


Then select "Not Now" from the drop-down menu.


From the drop-down option, choose why you want to delete your account.


Tap "Permanently terminated my account" after confirming your account password.


Select "OK."


How+to+delete+instagram+account+on+android Temporarily


If you need to delete your Instagram account for a few days for whatever reason, follow these steps to deactivate Instagram accounts temporarily.

Log in to your account and select the profile icon from the drop-down menu.

Select the Menu(three-line) option in the top-right corner.

Now select Settings (gear icon).

Select "Help" and then "Help Center."

Select Chrome as your browser.

Select Managing Your Account from the drop-down menu.

Select 'Delete Your Account' from the drop-down menu.

Select "How do I remove my account?" from the drop-down menu.

Now select "Delete your account" from the drop-down menu.

'Temporarily disable my account' should be selected.

Choose 'Why are you disabling your account?' as the reason.

Re-enter your password and select 'Temporarily disable account' from the drop-down menu.

Your account has now been canceled, and it will remain hidden from other users until you log in again.


How to download my Instagram data

Even if you want to remove your Instagram account permanently, you probably don't want to lose all of the photographs and videos you've contributed to the platform unless you're sure you have them stored elsewhere. As a result, you should probably download your data first.

If you only want to download a single image or movie, you can do so through third-party services. However, if you want to download everything, you can take the official approach and download all of your Instagram data in one giant zip file directly from Instagram. You may do this in the app or a browser, but we'll go over that technique first since you'll need to remove your Instagram account on a browser.

Log in to your account, click on your profile image, and select Settings > Privacy and Security from the drop-down menu. Click 'Request Download' under the 'Data Download' section. Click 'Next' after entering your email address. 


At this moment, you'll be prompted to re-enter your account password. After that, select 'Request Download.' After that, you'll get an email with a link to download your Instagram information. To obtain your data, click Download Data in the email and follow the instructions.
